About

Deinduomo Gesi


Hi, I’m Deinduomo Gesi a poet, photographer and the founder of The1920x. I studied Information Technology at the Federal University of Technology Owerri.


Pablo Picasso said ” Every child is an artist. The problem is how to remain an artist once he grows up”. This is one of my favourite quotes and what inspired the idea of creating the1920x. My vision for the1920x is to create a collective of creatives that will use photography, poetry, and music to tell a story so personal yet universal giving a voice to everyone who wishes to be heard.


The1920x currently comprises of me the photographer and two creative advisors, Stephanie and Victor who helps with curating the works thematically.


My goal is to win a Pulitzer prize for photography, please take a seat and watch me accomplish this goal.
